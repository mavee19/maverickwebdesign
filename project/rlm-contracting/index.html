RLM Contracting

Figma • Web Design

About the project

RLM Consult Certify Comply is an Australian consulting and recruitment firm specializing in compliance, certification, project management, workforce solutions, and regulatory support across construction, rail, infrastructure, transport, and other highly regulated industries. The business supports organizations operating in complex environments where trust, expertise, and compliance are critical to success.

The goal was to create a professional digital presence that clearly communicated RLM’s extensive service offering while reinforcing credibility and encouraging prospective clients to engage with the business.

Challenge

RLM provides a broad range of services spanning certification, compliance, consulting, project support, workforce solutions, and training.

Key considerations included:
• Presenting a large volume of information in a structured and approachable way
• Communicating expertise across multiple industries and service categories
• Establishing trust and credibility with prospective clients
• Highlighting the breadth of the company’s capabilities without overwhelming visitors
• Creating clear pathways for enquiries and lead generation

The website needed to balance detailed service information with a user experience that remained intuitive and easy to navigate.

Approach

The design focused on creating a clear information hierarchy that allowed visitors to quickly understand RLM’s services, industry expertise, and value proposition.

Content was organized into structured sections that progressively introduced the company’s capabilities, credentials, services, client relationships, and success stories. This approach helped simplify complex information while maintaining clarity throughout the user journey.

Particular attention was given to trust-building elements such as industry experience, certifications, client logos, testimonials, and service highlights to reinforce credibility and support conversion-focused decision making.

Results

The final website successfully unified multiple business offerings into a single premium digital experience.

Key outcomes included:
• Improved clarity across multiple service categories
• Stronger presentation of expertise and industry credibility
• Structured content that supports user understanding and navigation
• Clear lead-generation opportunities throughout the website
• Consistent visual presentation across service and informational content
• A professional online presence aligned with the organization’s reputation and growth objectives
