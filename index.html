How I Work

01.

Understand the Brand

Every project starts with understanding the business, audience, and brand direction.

I review existing brand assets, logos, questionnaires, stakeholder input, and competitive landscape to identify opportunities and establish a clear visual direction.

02.

Explore & Refine with AI

I use AI-assisted workflows to accelerate ideation, moodboard creation, competitive analysis, and visual exploration.

AI helps generate possibilities faster, but every recommendation is reviewed, curated, and refined to ensure the final direction remains strategic, distinctive, and aligned with the brand.

03.

Build the Design System

Before designing pages, I establish a scalable foundation.

Using Figma Variables, design tokens, and reusable components, I create a structured system that ensures consistency across the entire experience while making development more efficient.

04.

Design the Experience

With the system in place, I design responsive user experiences focused on usability, conversion, and clarity.

Every screen is built using reusable components rather than isolated mockups, ensuring consistency and scalability from the start.

05.

Align Design with Development

I design with implementation in mind.

Design tokens, components, spacing, and interactions are structured to align with the development platform, reducing handoff friction and creating a shared source of truth between design and code.

06.

Validate & Refine

The final experience isn’t complete until it matches the design intent.

I review implementation across devices and breakpoints, identify inconsistencies, and refine details to ensure the final product feels polished, performant, and production-ready.
